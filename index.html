📦 Backup & Restore — Data Continuity
Download your complete dataset as a backup before making changes to the platform. After upgrading, you can restore the data so analytics continue uninterrupted. Recommended: take a backup before every code update or new release.
What gets backed up: Users, Invoices, Designs, Events, Daily Tracker entries, Attendance, Roster, Revenue Log, Targets, Team — everything in localStorage. Google Sheet data is preserved separately on Google's servers (acts as a second backup automatically).
